Connect Your CCTV System

How it works: Connect your IP CCTV cameras to enable real-time search across live footage. The system will capture 15 seconds of footage from each connected camera for processing.

Setup Instructions

Step 1: Find Camera IP
Access your router settings (usually 192.168.1.1)
or check your camera documentation
Step 2: Access Camera Web
Open browser and go to:
http://<camera-ip>
Step 3: Login
Default credentials (varies by brand):
Username: admin
Password: 12345 or check manual
Step 4: Get RTSP URL
Find "Network" or "Streaming" settings
Look for RTSP URL/stream path
Step 5: Paste URL Below
Format: rtsp://username:password@ip:port/path

Common RTSP URLs:

Hikvision: /Streaming/Channels/101
Dahua: /stream/main
Uniview: /stream/profile1
Axis: /axis-media/media.amp
Reolink: /h264Preview_01_main
